Onadasail

Onadasail was a comprehensive project where I was entrusted with the end-to-end responsibility of creating a cohesive brand identity, designing the UI/UX, developing the WordPress website, and implementing SEO/SEM strategies.

The primary objective was to establish an effective online presence to start selling sailing experiences. Despite the multifaceted challenges, the focus remained on delivering a seamless online experience that generates reassurance for visitors, supported by responsive design to cater to a global audience.

UI/UX Design & Development, Branding.
